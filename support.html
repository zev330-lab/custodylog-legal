Common questions

Is my data private?
Yes. Incidents are stored encrypted on your device. The app does not require an account to use.

How do subscriptions work?
CustodyLog is free to log up to 25 incidents. A subscription unlocks unlimited incidents and the attorney/court/mediator report templates. Manage or cancel anytime in your device's Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions.

How do I export a report?
Open the Export tab and choose a report template (PDF), CSV, or a full JSON backup.
